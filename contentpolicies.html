Bushel Content Policy Guide

!Version one: Grok

Effective Date: March 31, 2025
Owned and Operated by Truevine Online LLC

Welcome to Bushel, a Christian social networking platform designed to connect churches, churchgoers, and believers in a safe, faith-centered environment. Our mission is to foster fellowship, encouragement, and spiritual growth rooted in biblical principles. To maintain a Christ-honoring community, we’ve established this Content Policy Guide. All users are expected to adhere to these standards, reflecting the values of love, purity, and respect found in Scripture (e.g., Philippians 4:8, Ephesians 4:29).

Guiding Principles

Bushel’s content policies are grounded in Christian teachings, emphasizing holiness, modesty, and edification. We aim to create a space where users can freely share their faith without compromising biblical integrity. Content that conflicts with these values, including anything that could be loosely considered "adult," is strictly prohibited.

Content Standards

1. General Conduct

Encouragement and Edification: Posts, comments, and interactions must uplift others and align with Christian love and kindness (Colossians 3:12-14).

No Profanity or Vulgarity: Language must remain clean and free of swearing, crude references, or suggestive terms.

Respectful Dialogue: Disagreements should be handled with grace and humility, avoiding hostility or personal attacks (Proverbs 15:1).

2. Prohibited Content

The following content is not permitted on Bushel under any circumstances:

Adult or Inappropriate Material:

No nudity, sexual references, suggestive imagery, or discussions of explicit topics (1 Thessalonians 4:3-5).

No content promoting lust, adultery, or immorality, even if subtle or implied.

Violence and Gore: No depictions of graphic violence, harm, or cruelty.

Substance Abuse: No promotion or glorification of illegal drugs, excessive alcohol use, or intoxication.

Hate Speech: No content that attacks individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, gender, or other traits (Galatians 3:28).

False Teachings: No promotion of doctrines or beliefs that contradict core Christian tenets (e.g., the Trinity, salvation through Christ – John 14:6).

Spam or Commercial Solicitation: No unsolicited advertising, scams, or unrelated promotions.

Faith-Based Guidelines

Scriptural Alignment: Content should reflect or be compatible with biblical teachings. Discussions of faith must remain respectful and avoid blasphemy or mockery of God, Jesus Christ, or the Holy Spirit (Exodus 20:7).

Modesty: Photos, videos, and text should reflect Christian modesty in appearance and intent (1 Timothy 2:9-10).

Church Focus: Content should support the mission of connecting churches and believers—e.g., sharing sermons, events, prayer requests, or testimonies.

Media Uploads

Images and Videos: Must be family-friendly, free of suggestive or inappropriate elements, and relevant to Christian community-building.

Documents: PDFs, text files, or other uploads (e.g., Bible studies) must align with Bushel’s standards and avoid prohibited topics.

Enforcement

Truevine Online LLC reserves the right to review and moderate all content on Bushel. Violations of this policy may result in:

Content Removal: Immediate deletion of offending posts, comments, or media.

Account Suspension: Temporary or permanent suspension for repeated or severe violations.

Reporting: Users can flag content for review by our moderation team, ensuring a swift response.

User Responsibilities

Self-Monitoring: Post only what honors God and builds up the Bushel community.

Reporting Violations: Notify us of content that breaches this policy via the “Report” feature.

Account Security: Protect your account to prevent misuse by others.

Appeals Process

If your content is removed or your account is restricted, you may appeal by submitting a ticket within 14 days. Provide your username, a description of the issue, and an explanation based on this policy. Our team will review and respond within 7 business days.

Updates to This Policy

Truevine Online LLC may revise this Content Policy Guide as needed. Users will be notified of significant changes via email or platform announcement. Continued use of Bushel implies acceptance of the updated terms.

Contact Us

For questions or clarification, reach out via ticket. Let’s work together to make Bushel a light to the world (Matthew 5:16).
